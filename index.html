Introduction

This website serves as a comprehensive repository for the Aerial Robotics Society at IIT Madras. It functions like a specialized Wikipedia, compiling resources, experiments, and research conducted at AeroClub IIT Madras.

Purpose: The purpose of this platform is to provide easy access to valuable information and documentation to foster learning, collaboration, and innovation in aerial robotics.
